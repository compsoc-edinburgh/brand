For the EGM, we're looking for enthusiastic individuals to take on the roles of Tech Secretary and Treasurer.
  • Treasurer: Manage our finances and play a key role in budgeting and financial planning.
  • Tech Secretary: Oversee our technical services, including server management and technical setup for events.
How to Get Involved: Interested in running for a position?
  • Submit a Manifesto: Write a manifesto no more than 200 words and email it in PDF format to committee@comp-soc.com by January 30th, 18:00. Include your name and desired position in the email subject and PDF.
